Current Project

Deep Dive into Weinberg’s Cosmology
My current research focuses on a detailed exploration of Weinberg’s *Cosmology*. Under the mentorship of Dr. Ali Akbar Abolhasani, I am revisiting foundational cosmological theories, refining our understanding of the early universe, and conducting numerical derivations that link theory to observation.

Previous Project

Python Development for Iran National Observatory
During my work at the Iran National Observatory, I developed Python-based tools to support astronomical observations. Inspired by methodologies at the European Southern Observatory, I created algorithms for data analysis and tools like a Sky Magnitude Calculator and Ephemeris Generator. This work combined my passion for coding with the practicalities of large-scale observational systems.
